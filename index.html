On Call app

  • #Healthcare
  • #iOS

Doing research about the spanish healthcare system I realized that during the on call shift doctors from all over the country rely on pieces of paper to remember the patients that they have yet to attend.

After talking with doctors about the pain points of this approach I designed an easy to use app to not only register the patients to attend, but also to check quick information that took precious time from them and to add features that adjusted to already existing behaviors like saving cases to follow up in the following days.
